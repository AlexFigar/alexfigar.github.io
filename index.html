Development Portfolio

A quick changelog of the projects I have been working on.

Interstellar Exploration - Shoreline Detection

Oceans have previously had no idea where land was in relation to waves so they would often clip into the terrain mesh. The ocean system samples the terrains heightmap to determine how large a wave should be. What I've currently implemented is very basic but it works for now.


Turkey Bot - Complete Re-Write

I didn't think I would be adding, removing and changing servers so much when I originally made the Turkey Bot, I also didn't think it would be popular with people from other discord servers. As a result I have completely re-written the bot to use json configs to control the number of servers and the ports for each server.


The Party Racing Game - Lobby System

Players can connect to a "garage" (lobby) to choose a car, car colour and track.
