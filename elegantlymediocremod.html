The Elegantly Mediocre Mod

An elegantly mediocre mod by an elegantly mediocre developer that adds elegantly mediocre content to an elegantly mediocre game.

My friends and I got back into minecraft for a few weeks before losing interest however during that time I took a break from Interstellar Exploration and created this mod which just adds a couple of custom items that my friends and I wanted. This was actually a lot of fun to make but it would have been even more fun if Java wasn't so painful to use.
